## FAQ\
\
### How do I add a player?\
Go to the "Squad" tab and tap the + button.\
\
### How do I save a match?\
Place your players on the pitch, tap "Match" and fill in the opponent name.\
\
### Is my data saved?\
Yes, automatically via iCloud if enabled on your device.\
\
### How do I create balanced teams?\
Go to the "Teams" tab, select available players, and tap "Generate".\
